About!

Hi! I'm a Senior Computer Science Major at Chapman University. I'm interested in software development and front-end design/development.

Outside of programming, music plays a huge part of my life. I spend a lot of my free time listening, discovering, talking about, and writing about music. You can find a lot of my ramblings on my blog.

I'm also a Education Director for Chapman Radio, Chapman University's student-run online radio station. In addition to being a staff member for the organization, I also produce and host my own radio show on the site, titled Pauly's Pocket, a project I am incredibly fond of.

I also enjoy graphic design and digital media creation with tools like Adobe Photoshop and Premiere Pro, two tools I use to produce my radio show.
